# Pagify 🌐

**From idea to deployed website — powered by AI agents.**

Pagify is an open-source toolkit that helps technically-capable coaches and volunteers guide non-technical people through building and launching their own websites. No more getting stuck halfway. No more abandoned projects.

---

## The Problem

Too many people attend website creation workshops inspired and motivated — then go home and never ship. The gap isn't desire. It's the gap between *learning* and *doing*, and that gap is where most people fall.

Pagify closes that gap.

---

## What Pagify Does

Pagify uses AI agents to take someone from a raw idea to a live, production-ready website:

- **Idea capture** — structured intake to understand purpose, audience, and goals
- **Content generation** — AI-drafted copy, structure, and page hierarchy
- **Traditional multi-page architecture** — no SPAs; real pages that search engines and AI engines can crawl and index
- **SEO-ready** — semantic HTML, meta tags, structured data, sitemap
- **GEO-ready** — Generative Engine Optimization; structured for AI search (Perplexity, ChatGPT, etc.)
- **One-command deployment** — GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Vercel

---

## Who This Is For

**Primary users (the coaches):** People with some technical background who want to help others — community coaches, volunteers, mentors, team leaders running website workshops.

**End beneficiaries:** Non-technical people who want a real web presence but can't navigate the tools alone.

---

## Philosophy

- **Traditional over trendy** — Multi-page sites beat SPAs for SEO, accessibility, and longevity
- **AI does the heavy lifting** — Agents handle the tedious parts; humans make the decisions
- **Open source forever** — This knowledge belongs to the community
